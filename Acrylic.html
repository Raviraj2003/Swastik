At Swastik Acrylic, we specialize in creating durable, stylish, and weather-resistant acrylic signs that elevate your brand’s presence. From office boards to shopfront signs, every design reflects precision and quality craftsmanship.

Custom Acrylic Sign Boards for Your Brand

Acrylic sign boards offer a premium, modern look that instantly enhances any commercial or corporate space. Whether you need 2D lettering, 3D cutouts, LED-backlit signs, or laser-engraved boards, Swastik Acrylic delivers perfection in every project.

  • Weatherproof and long-lasting materials
  • High-precision laser cutting and UV printing
  • LED and neon lighting options available
  • Custom sizes, fonts, and finishes

We handle everything from concept to installation — ensuring smooth edges, clean designs, and bright visuals that grab attention day and night.

Contact us today to discuss your design ideas or get a free quote. Let your signage speak volumes about your business with Swastik Acrylic.

2D Acrylic Boards

Our 2D acrylic boards are designed to give your brand a sleek and professional look. Crafted with precision laser cutting and UV printing technology, they are ideal for offices, showrooms, and retail stores. These boards are durable, weather-resistant, and available in various finishes and color combinations to match your brand identity.

  • Smooth and polished finish
  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor use
  • Fully customizable design options

3D Acrylic Boards

Add depth and dimension to your signage with our 3D acrylic boards. Perfect for corporate offices, malls, and branding displays, these boards create a strong visual impact with raised lettering and layered designs. We use top-quality acrylic sheets combined with precision-cut letters for a stunning 3D appearance.

  • Elegant raised design for premium look
  • Available in matte and glossy finishes
  • Long-lasting color and shine

LED Backlit Boards

Make your brand shine — literally! Our LED backlit boards feature energy-efficient LED lighting that highlights your brand even in low-light conditions. These boards are perfect for shopfronts, restaurants, and offices looking for a modern illuminated aesthetic.

  • Bright, uniform LED glow
  • Energy-efficient and long-lasting
  • Custom illumination colors available
